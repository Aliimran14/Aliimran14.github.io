Mastering Sequence Modeling with LSTMs

Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) is a specialized type of Recurrent Neural Network (RNN) designed to address the vanishing gradient problem. LSTMs excel at capturing long-term dependencies in sequential data. With their ability to selectively remember or forget information, they are widely used in natural language processing, time series forecasting. & other complex sequence modeling tasks.
